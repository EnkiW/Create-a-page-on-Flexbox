Deodorants are cosmetic products designed to mask, reduce or eliminate unpleasant odors. The mechanism of action of deodorants is to prevent the growth of bacteria in a humid environment and to absorb natural body odors and other intrusive odors. Deodorants often contain disinfectant and bactericidal additives that destroy microorganisms and prevent the appearance of an unpleasant odor.
